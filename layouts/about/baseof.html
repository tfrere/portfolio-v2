I lead front-end development for companies and organizations, building resilient UI foundations for their online experiences. I’ve worked on projects of different scales — from small micro-sites to larger Web applications.

I’m a design-minded front-end developer. Sometimes people who do what I do are also called front-end designers, UI developers, UI engineers, or design engineers. That is, my work lies at the intersection of design and back-end. I write HTML, CSS, SVG, and presentational JavaScript that powers Web user interfaces. With a focus on responsive design, semantic markup, and performance, I build resilient, inclusive foundations for user interfaces, marrying cutting-edge techniques with foundational Web standards. I collaborate with designers — preferrably in the design phase — to translate designs into live prototypes, resilient Web user interafces, and design systems. As a design engineer, I provide feedback to designers to ensure that the design translates well to the browser, ensuring usability of the product by as many people as possible, regardless of their context and how they use it (screen readers, mobile phones, various browsers, etc.)
